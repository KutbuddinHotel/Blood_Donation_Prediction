In this project, I worked on predicting whether a person is likely to donate blood based on their past donation history. I explored the dataset to understand patterns like how frequently people donate, how much they donate, and how recent their last donation was. After cleaning and preparing the data, I applied different machine learning models to predict the likelihood of future donations and compared their performance to find the most reliable one. This project helped me understand how behavioral patterns can be used in predictive modeling and how such insights can be useful for healthcare organizations to plan blood donation drives more effectively.
